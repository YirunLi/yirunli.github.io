About Me

Hello, I'm Yirun Li, a passionate engineer with over four years of experience in artificial intelligence, deep learning, and 3D reconstruction. I received my Master's degree in Computer Science in 2020 and Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering in 2017 from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. My expertise lies in leveraging cutting-edge technologies to create innovative solutions, optimize autonomous systems, and enhance data generation processes.

Currently, I work as a Simulation Algorithm Engineer at Dreame Technology, where I focus on synthetic data generation, robotic simulation, and 3D reconstruction. My work has enabled significant advancements in machine learning training datasets and simulation-based algorithm development. With a strong foundation in Python, C++, and frameworks like PyTorch and TensorRT, I am driven to tackle complex challenges and create impactful solutions in the AI and robotics fields.
